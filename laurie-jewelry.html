Jewelry Care

Your Laurie pieces are crafted to last a lifetime. With proper care, they will retain their brilliance for generations.

Daily Wear

Remove jewelry before exercising, swimming, or applying lotions and perfumes. Chemicals can dull the finish and affect gemstones.

Cleaning

Gently clean with a soft-bristled brush, warm water, and mild dish soap. Pat dry with a lint-free cloth.

Storage

Store each piece separately in the provided pouch or box to prevent scratching. Keep away from direct sunlight and humidity.

Diamonds

Diamonds attract grease. Clean regularly and have them professionally inspected once a year to check prong settings.

Gold

Polish gold with a soft cloth to maintain shine. Avoid contact with chlorine bleach which can weaken gold alloys.

Professional Service

We offer complimentary annual cleaning and inspection for all Laurie pieces. Book an appointment at our atelier.
